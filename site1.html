Pick Orange Over Other Fruits

As you stroll around the stalls of the fruit market, a store vendor at your nearest vinicity beckons you to take a look at his produce. After taking a quick glance about, you notice the various delicious and equally enticing pieces of fruits. The deep flush of tiny red strawberries tinted with specks of white seed is strangely placed next to a gaint round watermelon with beautifully carved strips of green and black. Grapes of different colors glitter from the light of the sun and catches your eyes. But do not let these inferior fruits beguile you with their mere appearance and spoil you from the taste of true deliciousness. Lying humbly at the rear of the stall is the oranges and they are the master of all fruits.
